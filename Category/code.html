Trump to sign Department of War order and live updates on administration announcements

By Kathryn Watson, Caitlin Yilek, Melissa Quinn
September 5, 2025 / 10:52 AM EDT • CBS News

President Trump is signing an executive order Friday to begin the process of renaming the Department of Defense to the Department of War, bringing back the name that was last used for the agency in the late 1940s.

His order is expected to reverse the naming decision that called the department the Department of Defense since 1947, when the National Security Act consolidated the War Department and the Department of the Navy and created the U.S. Air Force, unifying them under one agency for military affairs.
